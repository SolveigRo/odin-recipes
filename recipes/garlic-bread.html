Garlic Bread

Description

Easy and delicious way of making garlic bread by using a regular baguette.

Ingredients

  • 1 baguette
  • 100 g butter, room temperature
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 4 tbsp parsley, finely chopped

Steps

  1. Cut the baguette in sections, then slice them in the middle.
  2. Mix butter with crushed garlic cloves and parsley.
  3. Spread a good layer on each baguette piece.
  4. Put the pieces on a baking pan and cook them in the oven at 200 C for 10-15 minutes. They are done when golden at the edges.
  5. Serve the bread warm and fresh.
